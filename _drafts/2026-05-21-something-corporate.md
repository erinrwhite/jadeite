This March, after almost three years in civic tech, I started my next professional thing. I’m a UX Director for design engineering (aka front-end web development) at a large company headquartered in Rhode Island. It’s my first corporate gig. I’m back in an office most days of the week and I’m leading a team again after a few years as an individual contributor. 

Corporate is in many ways exactly as pictured on TV, and it’s also similar to my past lives in higher ed and the federal government: large, complex, occasionally inscrutable, and also home to kind folks who care about doing good work and taking care of each other along the way. This move was absolutely not on my bingo card, but neither was any other other stuff we’ve all lived through in the past 10 years or so.
